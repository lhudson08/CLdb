Welcome to CLdb (CRISPR Locus database)

This set of tools is designed to help you manage and analyze information on CRISRPs from a large number of genomes.

Main features

CRISPR Array BLAST (spacers & direct repeats)

  • Quickly and easily run BLASTn queries with your spacers and direct repeats.
  • Filter out spacer BLAST hits to CRISPR arrays.
  • Get the protospacer and PAM regions of each spacer BLAST hit.
    • Easily make weblogos for these features
  • Get alignments of the protospacer and DNA template of the crRNA (crDNA)
    • Get summaries of mismatches for the SEED and non-SEED regions

Plotting CRISPR loci

  • Make detailed plots comparing many CRISPR loci (both CAS genes and CRISPR arrays).
    • Compare CAS gene conservation (sequence similarity & position) among loci
    • Compare spacer content among loci
